# Level Overview

This document describes every playable level in sequence order.

## 1) DAY (Theme: DAY, Width: 96)
Baseline opener with simple platform gaps, early collectibles, and low-pressure enemy placement. Designed to teach movement, jumping rhythm, and help-block discovery.

## 2) AFTERNOON (Theme: AFTERNOON, Width: 96)
Adds denser coin lines and wider platform groupings. Introduces more mid-air navigation and slightly busier enemy timing than DAY.

## 3) JUNGLE (Theme: JUNGLE, Width: 96)
Focuses on staggered platforms and repeated jump chains. Enemy spacing starts to require cleaner approach angles and momentum control.

## 4) FACTORY (Theme: FACTORY, Width: 96)
Tighter routes with vertical punctuation and mixed obstacle pacing. Encourages quick path decisions while maintaining moderate difficulty.

## 5) CASTLE (Theme: CASTLE, Width: 96)
A mood shift with more aerial pressure and stricter timing windows. Platform placements reward deliberate jumps over speed-only play.

## 6) ICE (Theme: ICE, Width: 96)
Lower gravity and slippery feel increase commitment on movement choices. Players manage drift, spacing, and corrected landings under pressure.

## 7) VOLCANO (Theme: VOLCANO, Width: 96)
Adds lava hazards and heavier risk/reward routing. Mistakes are punished faster, making route discipline and jump timing central.

## 8) SKY RUINS (Theme: SKYRUINS, Width: 96)
Built to support the Ranger grapple fantasy with vertical visual motifs and airborne structure. Fast traversal and reaction play are emphasized.

## 9) NITE (Theme: NITE, Width: 96)
Dark-sky atmosphere with balanced challenge pacing before the long-form stages. Keeps pressure steady while preserving readability.

## 10) MOONLIT MATSURI (Theme: JAPAN, Width: 192)
First extended map (2x width). A long scenic run with multi-phase flow, broader platform variety, and sustained endurance pacing.

## 11) SPACE (Theme: SPACE, Width: 96)
Distinct movement feel with lower gravity and flyer-heavy lanes. Positioning and enemy behavior tracking become more important than raw speed.

## 12) CELESTIAL SHOGUNATE (Theme: JAPAN, Width: 288)
Three-act saga (3x width) with checkpoint progression.
- Checkpoint 1: Act 2: Storm Ascent
- Checkpoint 2: Act 3: Moon Fortress
A long challenge arc that escalates complexity while reducing replay friction through mid-level respawns.

## 13) PHANTOM PROCESSION (Theme: HORROR, Width: 384)
Four-act finale (4x width) with moonlit horror atmosphere, ambient howl layer, and skull-themed 1UP presentation.
- Checkpoint 1: Act 2: Grave March
- Checkpoint 2: Act 3: Lantern Hollow
- Checkpoint 3: Act 4: Eclipse Keep
Progresses from readable onboarding to denser endgame pressure, with coin lines guiding safer and riskier paths.

---

## Notes
- All levels use 18 tile rows.
- Standard levels are width 96; extended saga levels are width 192/288/384.
- Themes drive visuals, collectible styling, physics profile, and music behavior.
